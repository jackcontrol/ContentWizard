VINYL MASTERING INFO VINYL MASTERING INFO PRESSING THROUGH HELIOS PRESS Helios Press handles the vinyl manufacturing side for records that are ready to move from approved masters into production. Their team focuses on domestically manufactured vinyl records, physical-format quality, clear communication, and pressing logistics for independent artists, labels, and collectors. INDEPENDENT MUSIC FOCUSED Helios is operated by people with deep involvement in independent music, underground records, labels, publishing, and physical-format culture. That background makes the pressing path a natural fit for artists who care about the full record experience, not just the file delivery. DEDICATED VINYL PRESSING EQUIPMENT Helios uses manual vinyl pressing machines for 160-gram and 180-gram 12-inch records, with a range of color options available depending on the project and production details. BASED IN BRADY, TEXAS Helios Press is based in Brady, Texas, and handles the manufacturing details after the record is ready for pressing. CLEAR PRESSING COMMUNICATION The pressing stage involves quantities, color choices, jackets, inserts, test pressings, timelines, and delivery logistics. Helios is the correct path once your approved masters and production details are ready to move into manufacturing.
