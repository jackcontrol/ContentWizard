I have basically never looked back since.” Thomas Eriksen — Mork Pricing FAQ What is a Single-Format Master? A Single-Format Master is for one primary release format. That can be streaming/digital, vinyl, CD/DDP, cassette, digital download, MP3, or another confirmed delivery format. What is the Full Release Package? The Full Release Package is for releases that need multiple masters across physical and digital formats. It can include standard delivery masters for streaming, vinyl, CD/DDP, cassette, digital download, and MP3, depending on the confirmed release plan. Is one master enough for vinyl and streaming? Sometimes, but not always. Vinyl, streaming, CD/DDP, cassette, and digital delivery can have different practical requirements. If you are releasing in more than one format, choose the Full Release Package so the masters can be prepared correctly. What is Beyond Heavy? Beyond Heavy is the premium Jack Control and Maor Appelbaum mastering path for release-ready heavy mixes that need elite depth, weight, polish, translation, and all-format delivery. What files should I send? Send stereo WAV or AIFF files at the native sample rate and bit depth of the mix. Avoid lossy files, unnecessary conversion, and over-limited mix exports.
